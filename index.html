Tips

1: 🏆 Create tiers: Enter a name and hit "Add Tier"

2: 🖼️ Add images: Paste a URL and click "Add Image"

3: 🖱️ Organize: Drag and drop images into tiers

4: ↩️ Remove from tier: Double-click an image

5: 🗑️ Delete tier: Click the trash icon

6: ✏️ Rename tier: Hit the edit button

7: ❌ Permanently delete image: Click on image and press 'R'
